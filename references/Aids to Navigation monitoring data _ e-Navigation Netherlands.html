Aids to Navigation monitoring data

Introduction/Additional information: 
Monitoring the status of lighthouse equipment and buoys; reliability, maintenance, availability
Permitted as from: 
15/01/2009
Registrant: 
UK Trinity House
Message number: 
6
DAC: 
235
FI: 
10
Technical point of contact: 
Alan Stuwart
State: 
in force
Number of slots (max): 
1
Reporting Rate: 

Varies, depending on the type of AtoN (1-10 minutes)

The interval between the transmission of these messages will be synchronized with message 21, although not necessarily at the same reporting rate. If Message 21 is not used at a particular site, then the reporting interval should be selected to minimise the power requirement of the transponder, whilst still providing enough data to enable meaningful diagnostic analysis.

Used by: 
UK Trinity House
Commissioners of Irish Lights
Northern Lighthouse Board
How portrayed: 
Text and graphical (at shore-based monitoring centres, buoy tending vessels) - uses ICAN “Bespoke” dedicated graphic software for display - shows trending over time; alarm dispatching, etc.
